Guacamole

Source

TThis guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes. Serve with homemade tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Diced onion
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Minced garlic

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
